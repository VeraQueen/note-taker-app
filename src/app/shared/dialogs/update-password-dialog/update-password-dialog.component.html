Important: Updating Your Password

When you update your password, it becomes your new sign-in password for accessing our platform.

Here's what you need to know:

  1. Automatic Log Out: For security reasons, once you confirm the change, you'll be automatically logged out of your account. This is a standard security measure to ensure that only you have access to your account with the updated password.
  2. What to Do Next: After logging back in with your new password, you'll have full access to your account as usual.

Thank you for helping us maintain the security of your account.
