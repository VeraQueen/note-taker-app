Important: Updating Your Sign-In Email

When you update your email address, it becomes your new sign-in email for accessing our platform.

Here's what you need to know:

  1. Automatic Log Out: For security reasons, once you confirm the change, you'll be automatically logged out of your account. This is a standard security measure to ensure that only you have access to your account with the updated email.
  2. Verification Process: After updating your email, we'll send a verification code to the new email address you provided. Simply click the link to confirm the change.
  3. What to Do Next: After confirming the change and logging back in with your new email address, you'll have full access to your account as usual.

Thank you for helping us maintain the security of your account.
